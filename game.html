Rules :

  • You will be given a small piece of text that will be visible on your screen while you’re typing.
  • Your goal is to type it out as fast as you can, more importantly, type it correctly as fast as you can.
  • In case if character in text and character you typed out doesn’t match, the cursor wouldn’t be allowed to move further.
  • The mismatched character will be displayed in RED colour.
  • Once you have finished writing, your typing speed will be calculated and displayed.
